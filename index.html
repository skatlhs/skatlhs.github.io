Frequently Asked Questions

What's Barkly?

Barkly is a user to user app that helps people find their next pets. We show you listings of all types of pets, mostly dogs, in your area (state or province).

We have listings from individual sellers, breeders, or shelters nearby. You can also find cats, reptiles, fish, and toys, accessories, and equipment, for your pets as well.

What Can I List?

If you want to list something, we have a bunch of categories for you. We have a little over 50 categories for specific dog breeds, as well as categories for cats, reptiles, fish, other dog breeds we don\'t have listed, as well as toys, accessories & equipment for your pets.

How Do I List Something?

While you are using the app, you have 4 main navigation tabs on the bottom of your screen. They are: Location, Categories, Other, and Account. Head over to the account tab, and click on manage listings. You will then be brought to a page where you can access everything you have posted. To create a new listing, tap the blue button with a plus (+) icon.

If you want to edit a listing, simply tap the one you want to edit!

How Can I Contact Someone?

Interested in making a purchase? Each listing on barkly has an individual post page. If you see something you like while scrolling, tap the listing to view more details on the item. From that page you can contact the seller and start a conversation with them. You can discuss more details privately with the seller.

Not Ready To Buy

If you see something you like, you can save it by clicking the heart icon on the post page. The heart icon is located in the top right corner.

Click on the Account tab, then click 'Saved Postings' to view everything you have saved.

How Are Posts Filtered?

We show you listings based on your state or province, and by default we show the newest ones first.

If you select an individual category from the 'Categories' tab on the main navigation page, you can sort by price: highest and lowest, or reset it back to default (newest).
